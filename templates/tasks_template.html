Novice Quests

Novice quests are simple tasks designed for beginners. Each problem you solve will give you 30XP.

Adventurous Challenges

Adventurous challenges offer a moderate level of difficulty, suitable for members with a bit of experience. These tasks involve data structures, basic algorythms, more complex logical operations. For each problem you will be awarded with 60XP.

Epic Campaigns

Epic campaigns are high-stakes and challenging quests that demand advanced skills. You will have to solve complex algorythms, work with OOP principles and advanced data structures. Each task will give you 100XP.

Abyssal Trials

BE ADVISE! You will have only one chance per week to submit your solution! If your solution is not 100% correct you will loose XP, the amount of points which is not enough to finish your task.

Abyssal Trials are the most challenging quests in the realm, reserved for the bravest and most experienced adventurers. These nightmare-level tasks plunge players into the depths of chaos and require unparalleled skill, strategy, and determination to overcome.
